Overview

I'm participating in several research activities, mostly in two domains: the methodology of cartographic design and the geography of science.

Following my doctoral work on the means and ways to better integrate aesthetics into cartographic design, I participated in several conferences and produced some papers.

My other research interest, the geography of science, stem from the "Geoscience" ANR research project in 2009-2013, followed by two Labex projects and currently expanding into european projects.
